# Creating a Zero Trust Visual Using AI + Canva

As I continue building in cybersecurity, I’ve been exploring how to better understand and communicate complex concepts.

For this, I used AI-generated code in Canva to create a visual breakdown of Zero Trust, and then published it as a live interactive.

## Approach

Instead of just reading about the concept, I translated it into a simple visual model:

- 🔒 Protected resource at the center  
- 👤 User, 💻 Device, 🌐 Network, ☁️ Application surrounding it  
- 🔁 “Verify” at every connection  

## Key Insight

Zero Trust is often explained as:

**“Never trust - always verify.”**

What stood out to me is that it’s not just a technical framework - it represents a shift in how we think about access, identity, and risk, especially in distributed and cloud-based environments.

## Why this matters

This kind of translation is important for:
- Security awareness  
- Communication across technical and non-technical teams  
- Making risk more understandable and actionable
